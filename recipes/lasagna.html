Lasagna

Description

Italian dish which tastes very yummy but is fiendish to make

Ingredients

  • 500g sheet pasta
  • 300g beef mince
  • Tin tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Olive oil
  1. Fry oniions in oil
  2. Add beef and stir until browned
  3. Add tin tomatoes, stir and leave to simmer
  4. Layer a third of the mixture in a shallow dish
  5. Place sheets of pasta on top, continue layering
  6. Chuck in oven and hope for the best
  7. Oh, I forgot the bechamel sauce :(
