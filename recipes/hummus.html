Hummus

Description

Middle Eastern dish often served with a tomato and cucumber salad.

Ingredients

  • Tin of Chickpeas
  • Tahini paste, 2 tablespoons
  • Juice of half a Lemon
  • Garlic, 3 cloves, peeled and roughly chopped
  • Olive oil, 2 tablespoons
  1. Place all ingredients in a food mixer and mix until the desirred consistency is reached
  2. Serve with diced tomatoes and cucumber
  3. Enjoy! Om nom nom
